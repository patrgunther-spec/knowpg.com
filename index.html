🔍 WHO ARE WE?

Welcome to Garth Pizza Club

We are a crew of pizza lovers, music heads, and creative misfits.

Founded in the spirit of late-night slices, loud speakers, and good company. Garth Pizza Club is where the love of food meets culture, community, and a whole lot of vibes.

Our Mission

Bring people together through pizza, music, and unforgettable experiences. Whether it's a pop-up event, a creative collab, or just sharing our favorite slice spots — we're here for all of it.

The Crew

We're a collective of creators, chefs, DJs, designers, and dreamers. Every member brings something different to the table (literally).
